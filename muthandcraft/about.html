Our Story

Built on Skill, Trust & Hard Work

Muth & Craft was founded on a simple idea: Connecticut homeowners deserve a single, dependable craftsman who can handle everything around the house — without juggling a dozen contractors.

For more than 15 years we've done exactly that. From small repairs to full remodels, from the plumbing behind the walls to the garden out front, we bring the same care and attention to every job. Whatever the customer envisions, we make it real.

  • Family-run, hands-on, and personally accountable
  • One crew for interior, exterior and everything between
  • Honest estimates and respectful, tidy work
  • Licensed & insured throughout Connecticut
