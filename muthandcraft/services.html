Good to Know

Frequently Asked Questions

What areas of Connecticut do you serve?

We serve homeowners across Connecticut, with a focus on Fairfield and New Haven counties — Stamford, Norwalk, Fairfield, Bridgeport, New Haven and surrounding towns. Not sure if you're in range? Reach out and ask.

Are estimates really free?

Yes. We provide a free, no-obligation written estimate after understanding your project. You'll know the scope, timeline and cost before any work begins.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes — Muth & Craft is fully licensed and insured in Connecticut. License details are provided on every written estimate and contract.

Can you handle both small repairs and big remodels?

Absolutely. From a single leaky faucet to a full home renovation with architectural plans, we scale to fit the job — and you deal with one accountable team the whole way.

Do you work from architectural plans?

Yes. We implement architectural plans and custom designs, and we can advise on practical details to bring your vision to life within budget.
